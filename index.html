What to Document Up There
1
Underlayment Condition
Check at exposed edges — cracking, brittleness, color degradation. Photograph with CompanyCam.
2
Tile / Shingle Integrity
Cracks, slippage, missing tiles, lifted edges. Note count and location.
3
Flashings
Pipe boots, step flashing, counter flashing, drip edge. These are the #1 leak source.
4
Deck Soft Spots
Walk carefully — note any flex or soft areas. Indicates OSB saturation or rot.
5
Overall Grade Call
A–F. Commit to a grade before you come back down. Don't hedge it in front of the homeowner.
